Land use

Principle of residential development

Complies

Policy references: London Plan H1, Southwark Plan ST1

The site is allocated for residential-led mixed use development under Site Allocation NSP45. The principle of housing is therefore supported in this location.

Loss of employment land

Needs changes

Policy references: Southwark Plan P30

The existing site comprises approximately 850 sqm of B1(c) light industrial floorspace. Policy P30 requires marketing evidence to demonstrate there is no reasonable prospect of the site being used for employment purposes before any loss can be supported.

The marketing evidence submitted to date is insufficient. To comply with policy, the applicant must demonstrate:

  • Active marketing for a continuous period of at least 2 years
  • Marketing at a reasonable market rent (not inflated)
  • Use of appropriate commercial agents
  • Evidence of enquiries received and why they did not proceed

Active ground floor uses

Complies

Policy references: Southwark Plan P35

The provision of 200 sqm Class E commercial floorspace at ground floor is welcomed and meets the requirements for active frontages on this designated shopping frontage.
